Final Year Project


In my Final Year Project, I have chosen to do a Data Science topic which is Summarisation ENews Article. The project summarise the needed information in enewspapers. In my project, I only touch on health relate article. Hence I focusing on diseases only and have to identify the percentage of the disease as well as the year the diseases are being recorded. To do that firstly I have to crawl news articles at the local newspaper website. Then take the necessary keywords to then input all the keywords into a ML application to enterprises a view in patterns to overcome or reduce the percentage of the diseases in Brunei Darussalam.


Coding.bn


Coding.bn is 6 months programme which are divided into 2 parts. Then we creating a projects in groups and showcase the projects to hosted companies.
For the second part is another 3 months period where us the participants are on-the-job training with the hosting companies with continuous mentorship throughout the whole duration.
